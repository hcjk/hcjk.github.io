The process.

Brilliantly crafted products.

  1. Research

    One of the most crucial and critical steps to the whole process is researching the product, competitors, and potential solutions. Because of this, I consider this the most important step in the process. Researching generally involves getting a stronger grasp on the product, what it does, what the issue is, and possible ways to improve it.


  2. Analysis

    Analysis provides a structure in which a product can be built upon. It is because it involves choosing the best solution for the product or task at hand.


  3. Design

    This step is the most important step to a site's users. By creating a beautiful and usabiliy focused design, I create a whole new level in which users can interact with. The first time a user visits a site, the first thing that they see is the design. I build designs that grasp a user's attention within the first few seconds of visiting.


  4. Development

    The development of a product is another incredibly important part of the process. With development, I create a seamless, easy to use site that is focused on being fast and efficient for the user. Similarly to design, the site needs to be swift and able to handle a large amount of traffic.


  5. =Tests

    A critical step to the process is testing, in other words, making sure that it works completely. I test on many devices and browsers to ensure a solid and fluid experience throughout.


  6. Integration

    Integrating is the final step of the process, but definitely an essential one. Integration is the process by which I implement the product that I've built into a user's product. I provide a smooth and easy integration without server drops or any latency for users.
