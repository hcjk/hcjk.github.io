intrn surfaces raw talent to startups that need it most.

ABSTRACT

intrn.co was created in order to help students and recent graduates find jobs at local startups without the hassle.

INTRODUCTION

Johnny Senglemann, a recent graduate from University of Denver, wanted a way for students to be able to get jobs at local startups. Two years later, Johnny was a an Uber driver, and happened to be driving Daniel Farrell and a few other student entrepreneurs to Boulder Startup Weekend. They exchanged business cards, and were able to later get in contact with Matt Zweibel, a community manager at Galvanize. Matt saw a similar issue, especially to those that were recent graduates of the Galvanize programs. However, given the coworking opportunities Galvanize provided people, he also saw it as a solution.
